ACCOMODATION

The National Workshop on “IACUC in Nigeria” is a non-residential event. Delegates are responsible for their accommodation for the duration of the workshop in Ibadan. Rooms are available at the IITA conference center on first come, first serve basis. However, Workshop Organizers have arranged opportunities with other Hotels nearby for the comfort of delegate. There will be shuttle transportation to and from the Hotels listed on this web page if delegates choice to reside there.

Accommodation should be booked directly and independently with the hotels online or by phone.

Kindly indicate during registration on arrival your transportation requirement.
