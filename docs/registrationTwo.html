MEMBER OF IACUC/AEC


Begin with entry of the Lead Delegation and make registration fee payment before you continue. Then continue to register other members of your institutional delegation up to a maximum of three (3) persons. You may return to make changes to your delegation membership until registration closes after which you cannot make changes neither can you make any substitution. Individuals above the allowed delegation should register as Non-delegation participants. Delegate registration is incomplete without registration fee payment.
