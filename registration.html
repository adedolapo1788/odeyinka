CANCELLATION POLICY

All cancellations must be in writing. Cancellations received until 1st September 2019 are subject to Bank and Credit card charges and a charge of 10% of the registration fees for administrative costs. Until 1st October 2019, will receive 50% refund subject to a charge of 10% of the registration fees for administrative costs. After 1st October 2019, full payment will be requested, and refunds will not be provided. Delegate substitution and replacements by colleagues, i.e. transfer of the registration at no extra cost, are much welcome until 1st October 2019.

Data Protection Statement & Personality / Image Rights

By filling out the registration form, the participant gives consent that ACURET can process the data provided within the framework of the conference and allow photographs to be made during the conference. This includes, unless registered participants object, all handling needed for the applicant’s participation at the event and for the drafting of a list of participants which will be distributed at the conference, and placing photographs in the pictures gallery accessible only by participants and ACURET members, in the RODENTIA newsletter or selecting some for articles on the conference in a journal or newspaper, or in any other web/printed publication.

Right of access: applicants have a right to access and ask for changing or deleting their personal data, which will be kept by ACURET.ORG. ACURET would like to contact you occasionally to keep you informed of future ACURET events and other relevant information. If you do not wish us to do this, please tick this box to be removed from our general distribution list.
